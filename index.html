About Us

JAYSAR SECURITY LLP

We provide manned guarding services across the state of Uttar Pradesh.We possess PSARA License issued by Govt Of Uttar Pradesh.Members of our Security Teams are trained at training institute,recognised by PSARA authorities.Further,their skills are polished under capable supervision and guidance of EX-Military Officers.Our trained and highly disciplined Security Teams comprising Field Officers,Supervisors and Security Guards are fully committed to protect the lives and properties of our valuable clients.

Our services

GUARDING PREMISES

Our Armed and Unarmed Security Personnel are trained to protect various diverse sites like Commercial Spaces,Hospitals,Educational Institutions,Factories,Malls,Residential Societies,Manufacturing units,Construction Sites etc

CCTV MONITORING

Since CCTV camera plays vital role in preventing and detecting personal and property crimes,hence we have a well trained and separate Team,which monitors CCTV cameras 24*7 vigilantly to prevent such mishappenings.

PERSONAL SECURITY

With growing threats and risks to personal safety and security,there is a dire need of Personal Protection these days.So we have a dedicated Team,comprising Armed and Unarmed Personnel,who are very well trained,physically strong and mentally robust,led by EX Army/Police Officials.

EVENT SECURITY

Our Security Agency understands the importance of proper event safety,security planning and the importance of effective emergency response.So we have a dedicated Team to ensure better crowd management,to keep check on uninvited guests and to activate emergency evacuation plan,in case of any emergency.We are committed to prevent the likelihood of injuries or property damages at any cost under any circumstance.

Why Choose Us

  • Every member of our Security Teams is vaccinated.
  • Complete background check along with Police Verification is done by our Agency before hiring the members for our Security Teams.
  • The physical standards issued by the Government are strictly followed during the recruitment of the Security Personnel for our Agency.
  • Apart from the training from PSARA authorised Training Institutes,our Agency provides additional training to the Security Personnel,under supervision of EX Army Officials.
  • Walkie talkies,earpieces etc are provided to our Security Teams to smoothen the flow of communication.
  • Our Security Team members are trained to use fire extinguishers and fire hydrants.In emergency like fire outbreak situations,they can also help people to evacuate safely and inform Fire Department regarding the incident.
  • Our Security Team members are trained to provide basic medical treatment like first aid,CPR etc.
  • Comprehensive counselling sessions by experts are provided to the members of Security Teams to polish their communication skills,so that they can behave in appropriate and respectful manner with women and senior citizens around them,while being on duty.
  • We take care of our Security Team Members very well,by giving them salary more than minimum wages authorised by Governments,which drives them to work with utmost integrity & dedication which ultimately serves our purpose to provide best services to our clients.
